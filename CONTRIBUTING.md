# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in contributing to n8n Docs!

All contribution guidance — how to submit changes, our writing standards, content
types, and the review process — lives in the **[Contribution Guide](https://docs.n8n.io/contributing/docs-contributing-guide.md)**.

A few quick pointers before you start:

- **This repo is for the documentation site only.** For product help, use the
  [forum](https://community.n8n.io/c/questions/12); for product bugs and feature
  requests, use the [n8n product repo](https://github.com/n8n-io/n8n).
- By contributing, you agree to our
  [Code of Conduct](https://github.com/n8n-io/n8n-docs/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)
  and [Contributor License Agreement](https://github.com/n8n-io/n8n-docs/blob/main/CONTRIBUTOR_LICENSE_AGREEMENT.md).
