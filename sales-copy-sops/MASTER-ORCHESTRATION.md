## Troubleshooting Guide

### Problem: Can't identify unique mechanism
**Solution:**
1. Return to Phase 2 - ask client specific questions
2. Research deeper in Phase 3 - look at why alternatives fail
3. Synthesize in Phase 4 - the mechanism is often hidden in patterns

### Problem: Weak proof/testimonials
**Solution:**
1. Request additional testimonials from client
2. Mine Amazon reviews for result language
3. Use logical proof and mechanism explanation
4. Strengthen guarantee to offset

### Problem: Copy feels generic
**Solution:**
1. Return to Master VOC - use more exact phrases
2. Add more specific details and stories
3. Strengthen the mechanism explanation
4. Include more proof throughout

### Problem: Research taking too long
**Solution:**
1. Focus on primary subreddits/products only
2. Use AI agents for initial extraction
3. Stop when patterns start repeating
4. Prioritize quality over quantity
