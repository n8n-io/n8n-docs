# SOP 1.2: Product Category Classification

## Purpose
Accurately classify the client's product to ensure appropriate research methodologies, copywriting frameworks, and compliance considerations are applied throughout the project.

---

## Inputs Required

| Input | Source | Required |
|-------|--------|----------|
| Product access or documentation | Task 1.1 | Yes |
| Client's description of product | Initial contact | Yes |
| Target market information | Client | Yes |

---

## Product Classification Matrix

### Primary Categories

| Category | Description | Sub-Categories |
|----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Digital Products** | Intangible products delivered electronically | Courses, SaaS, Memberships, Ebooks, Templates, Software |
| **Physical Products** | Tangible products shipped to customer | Supplements, Devices, Books, Equipment |
| **Services** | Ongoing work performed for clients | Coaching, Consulting, Agency Services, Done-for-You |
| **Hybrid** | Combination of above | Course + Coaching, Product + Service |

---

## Step-by-Step Procedure

### Step 1: Identify Primary Category
**[TIME: 5 minutes]**

Answer these questions to determine primary category:

**Question 1: What does the customer receive?**
- Digital files/access → **Digital Product**
- Physical item shipped → **Physical Product**
- Ongoing work/support → **Service**
- Combination → **Hybrid**

**Question 2: How is value delivered?**
- One-time purchase, self-serve → **Product**
- Ongoing relationship, personalized → **Service**
- Both → **Hybrid**

### Step 2: Identify Sub-Category
**[TIME: 5 minutes]**

#### For Digital Products:

| Sub-Category | Characteristics | Examples |
|--------------|-----------------|----------|
| **Online Course** | Structured curriculum, video lessons, modules | Masterclass, Cohort course, Self-paced course |
| **SaaS** | Software subscription, ongoing access, tool-based | Project management, CRM, Analytics |
| **Membership** | Ongoing access, community, regular content | Content library, Community membership |
| **Ebook/Guide** | One-time download, informational | PDF guides, Playbooks, Blueprints |
| **Templates/Tools** | Reusable assets, plug-and-play | Spreadsheets, Swipe files, Frameworks |
| **Software (one-time)** | Downloadable software, perpetual license | Desktop apps, Plugins, Extensions |

#### For Physical Products:

| Sub-Category | Characteristics | Examples |
|--------------|-----------------|----------|
| **Nutritional Supplement** | Ingestible, health claims, FDA regulated | Vitamins, Nootropics, Protein powders |
| **Health Device** | Physical device, health-related | Fitness trackers, Therapy devices |
| **Consumer Product** | General consumer goods | Home goods, Fashion, Electronics |
| **Information Product** | Physical but info-based | Printed books, Card decks, Journals |

#### For Services:

| Sub-Category | Characteristics | Examples |
|--------------|-----------------|----------|
| **1:1 Coaching** | Individual attention, transformation-focused | Life coaching, Business coaching, Health coaching |
| **Group Coaching** | One-to-many, cohort-based | Group programs, Masterminds |
| **Consulting** | Expert advice, strategic | Business consulting, Marketing consulting |
| **Agency/Done-for-You** | Work performed for client | Marketing agency, Design agency |
| **Freelance/Contractor** | Project-based work | Writing, Development, Design |

### Step 3: Identify Market Category
**[TIME: 5 minutes]**

Determine which broad market the product serves:

| Market | Sub-Markets | Copy Considerations |
|--------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Health & Fitness** | Weight loss, Muscle building, Longevity, Disease-specific | Compliance critical, Before/after restrictions, Medical claims |
| **Wealth & Business** | Make money online, Investing, Career, Entrepreneurship | Income claims, FTC compliance, Proof requirements |
| **Relationships** | Dating, Marriage, Family, Social skills | Emotional copy, Story-heavy |
| **Personal Development** | Productivity, Mindset, Spirituality, Skills | Aspiration-focused, Transformation stories |
| **Hobbies & Interests** | Photography, Music, Art, Sports | Passion-driven, Community-focused |
| **B2B** | Software, Services, Consulting | ROI-focused, Logic-heavy, Case studies |

### Step 4: Identify Compliance Requirements
**[TIME: 5-10 minutes]**

Based on classification, identify applicable compliance:

#### Health Products (Supplements, Health Devices, Health Coaching):
- [ ] FDA regulations for claims
- [ ] FTC truth in advertising
- [ ] No disease claims without approval
- [ ] Disclaimer requirements
- [ ] "Results not typical" considerations
- [ ] Before/after photo restrictions

#### Financial Products (Biz-op, Investing, Make Money):
- [ ] FTC income disclosure requirements
- [ ] No guaranteed income claims
- [ ] Average results disclosure
- [ ] "Results not typical" requirements
- [ ] Proof documentation for all claims

#### General:
- [ ] Testimonial substantiation
- [ ] Refund policy disclosure
- [ ] Clear and conspicuous disclosures
- [ ] CAN-SPAM compliance (for emails)
- [ ] Platform-specific ad policies

### Step 5: Determine Copy Approach
**[TIME: 5 minutes]**

Based on classification, identify the optimal copy approach:

| Product Type | Primary Approach | Key Elements |
|--------------|------------------|--------------|
| Digital Course | Transformation story | Before/after journey, curriculum reveal, bonuses |
| SaaS | Problem/Solution | Pain points, demo, features-benefits, ROI |
| Coaching | Results + Relationship | Testimonials, methodology, coach story |
| Supplement | Mechanism + Proof | Scientific backing, ingredient story, unique formula |
| Agency | Case Studies | Results, process, team credibility |

**[TIME: 10 minutes]**

## Classification Decision Trees

### Decision Tree 1: Primary Category

```
Is the product...
│
├── Delivered digitally (download/access)?
│   └── YES → DIGITAL PRODUCT
│       ├── Is it software with ongoing subscription? → SaaS
│       ├── Is it structured learning content? → COURSE
│       ├── Is it ongoing access/community? → MEMBERSHIP
│       └── Is it a one-time asset? → EBOOK/TEMPLATE
│
├── Shipped physically to customer?
│   └── YES → PHYSICAL PRODUCT
│       ├── Is it ingestible? → SUPPLEMENT
│       ├── Is it a health device? → HEALTH DEVICE
│       └── Other → CONSUMER PRODUCT
│
├── Performed by provider for customer?
│   └── YES → SERVICE
│       ├── Is it 1:1 transformation? → COACHING
│       ├── Is it strategic advice? → CONSULTING
│       └── Is it work done for them? → AGENCY/DFY
│
└── Combination of above?
    └── YES → HYBRID (identify components)
```

## Common Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Product doesn't fit neatly into one category | Use Hybrid classification; document both components |
| Unsure about compliance requirements | Default to MORE conservative; flag for legal review |
| New/novel product type | Classify by closest analog; note unique aspects |
| Client disagrees with classification | Document their preference but maintain compliance flags |
