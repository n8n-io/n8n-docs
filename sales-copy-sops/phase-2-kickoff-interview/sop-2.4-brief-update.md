## Purpose
Update the preliminary brief with kick-off call insights and create a prioritized research plan for Phase 3.

## Step-by-Step Procedure

### Step 1: Review All Post-Call Documents
**[TIME: 15-20 minutes]**

Before updating the brief, review:
- [ ] VOC Document - Customer language captured
- [ ] Mechanism Insights - Unique approach documented
- [ ] Story Assets - Stories identified
- [ ] Proof Inventory - Evidence cataloged
- [ ] Insights Summary - Key takeaways clear

Note any inconsistencies or new questions.

### Step 2: Update Brief to Version 1.0
**[TIME: 30-45 minutes]**

Take the Preliminary Brief and update every section with call insights.

```markdown
## Research Questions to Answer

### About the Customer
- [ ] What exact words do customers use to describe [pain point]?
- [ ] What triggers them to seek a solution?
- [ ] What have they tried before?
- [ ] What are they really afraid of?
- [ ] What does their dream outcome look like in detail?

### About the Problem
- [ ] Why do common solutions fail?
- [ ] What is the real underlying issue?
- [ ] What misconceptions do people have?
- [ ] How big/urgent is this problem?

### About the Solution
- [ ] What evidence supports the mechanism?
- [ ] What results are realistic/typical?
- [ ] What makes this approach unique?
- [ ] What objections need addressing?

### About the Market
- [ ] What are competitors doing well?
- [ ] What gaps exist that we can fill?
- [ ] What claims are being made?
- [ ] What's the general conversation?

---

## Research Output Requirements

For each research source, capture:

1. **Exact quotes** (verbatim language)
2. **Source link/reference**
3. **Context** (what prompted this comment)
4. **Category** (pain/desire/story/objection/etc.)
5. **Strength** (1-5 how useful for copy)
6. **Application** (where to use in copy)

## Success Criteria

Research is complete when:

- [ ] At least 50 exact customer quotes captured
- [ ] Pain points validated with external sources
- [ ] Desires validated with external sources
- [ ] Mechanism has supporting evidence
- [ ] Competitor gaps identified
- [ ] Story opportunities documented
- [ ] VOC document expanded
- [ ] Brief can be updated to v2.0
```

**Trigger:** Brief updated, gaps identified

**Instructions:**
```

research plan based on project gaps and needs.

1. IDENTIFY gaps:
   - Low-confidence areas
   - Missing proof
   - Unvalidated claims
   - Incomplete customer understanding

   - Impact on copy effectiveness (high impact first)

- Specific sources and queries
- Time estimates
- Success criteria
- Output requirements
```
