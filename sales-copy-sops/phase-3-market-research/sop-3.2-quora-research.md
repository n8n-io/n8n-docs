# SOP 3.2: Quora Research

## Purpose
Extract detailed questions, thoughtful answers, and nuanced discussions from Quora where people seek in-depth information and solutions.

---

## Why Quora?

Quora is valuable for copywriters because:
- Questions reveal exact concerns and knowledge gaps
- Answers show what information people find valuable
- Discussions are more thoughtful and detailed
- Expert perspectives provide authority angles
- Questions reflect the buyer's journey stage
- Language is clearer and more articulate

## Step-by-Step Procedure

### Step 1: Identify Relevant Quora Topics/Spaces
**[TIME: 15-20 minutes]**

#### Method A: Topic Search
1. Go to quora.com
2. Search for your topic
3. Click "Topics" tab in results
4. Follow relevant topics

#### Method B: Browse Related Topics
1. Go to a relevant topic page
2. Check "Related Topics" section
3. Follow the topic tree

#### Create Your Topic List
```markdown
## Target Quora Topics

### Primary Topics
1. [Topic name] - [Follower count] - [Relevance]
2. [Topic name] - [Follower count] - [Relevance]
3. [Topic name] - [Follower count] - [Relevance]

### Secondary Topics
1. [Topic name] - [Relevance]
2. [Topic name] - [Relevance]
```

### Step 2: Develop Search Queries
**[TIME: 10-15 minutes]**

Quora search works best with question-style queries:

#### Query Templates
```
"How do I [achieve desired outcome]?"
"Why can't I [problem they're having]?"
"What is the best way to [goal]?"
"Does [solution type] really work for [problem]?"
"What are the most common mistakes in [topic]?"
"Why do most people fail at [goal]?"
"What's the difference between [option A] and [option B]?"
"Is [common belief] true?"
"How long does it take to [achieve something]?"
"What should I avoid when [doing something]?"
```

### Step 3: Execute Searches
**[TIME: 2-3 hours]**

For each query:
1. Search on Quora
2. Review top 10-20 questions
3. Open promising questions in new tabs
4. Read top-voted answers

#### Evaluation Criteria
| Indicator | What It Means |
|-----------|---------------|
| High views | Common question |
| Many answers | Contested/complex topic |
| High-upvote answers | Resonant information |
| Expert answers | Authority potential |
| Recent activity | Current relevance |

## Advanced Quora Techniques

### Technique 1: Google Site Search
```
site:quora.com "[your search term]"
site:quora.com "[topic]" how to
site:quora.com "[topic]" why
```

### Technique 2: Answer Analysis
Look for answers from:
- Industry experts (check credentials)
- People with personal experience
- High-upvote answers (resonance indicator)
- Controversial answers (different perspectives)

### Technique 3: Question Mining
Questions reveal buyer journey stage:
- "What is [X]?" = Unaware/Problem aware
- "How do I [X]?" = Solution aware
- "Is [Product] good?" = Product aware
- "Which is better, X or Y?" = Most aware

### Technique 4: Comment Sections
Answer comments often contain:
- Additional questions
- Objections
- Personal stories
- Disagreements

**Instructions:**
```

find and extract questions, answers, and insights from Quora.

4. EXTRACT valuable answers:

CRITICAL REQUIREMENTS:
```
