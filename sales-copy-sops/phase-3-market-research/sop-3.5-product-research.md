# SOP 3.5: Product/Scientific Research

## Purpose
Research the product mechanism, scientific backing, and technical details to understand and articulate why the product works. Especially critical for supplements, health products, and products with technical claims.

---

## When This Task Applies

| Product Type | Priority | Focus |
|--------------|----------|-------|
| Supplements | HIGH | Ingredients, dosages, studies |
| Health devices | HIGH | Mechanism, clinical evidence |
| Health coaching | MEDIUM | Methodology, science backing |
| Digital courses | MEDIUM | Methodology validation |
| SaaS | LOW | Technical credibility |
| Coaching (general) | LOW | Framework validation |

---

| Input | Source | Required |
|-------|--------|----------|

## Step-by-Step Procedure

### FOR SUPPLEMENTS: Ingredient Research

### Step 1: Create Ingredient List
**[TIME: 10 minutes]**

```markdown
## Ingredients to Research

| Ingredient | Dosage | Client's Claim | Priority |
|------------|--------|----------------|----------|
| [Ingredient 1] | [Amount] | [What they claim it does] | High |
| [Ingredient 2] | [Amount] | [What they claim] | High |
| [Ingredient 3] | [Amount] | [What they claim] | Medium |
```

### Step 2: Research Each Ingredient
**[TIME: 30-60 min per key ingredient]**

#### Research Sources
| Source | Best For | Credibility |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| **PubMed** | Clinical studies | Highest |
| **Examine.com** | Supplement research summaries | High |
| **WebMD** | General health info | Medium |
| **Healthline** | Consumer-friendly explanations | Medium |
| **NIH Office of Dietary Supplements** | Official position | High |
| **Manufacturer studies** | Proprietary forms | Varies |

#### Research Questions for Each Ingredient
1. What does this ingredient do (mechanism of action)?
2. What benefits are supported by research?
3. What's the clinically effective dosage?
4. How does our dosage compare?
5. Are there any notable studies to cite?
6. What's unique about this ingredient?
7. Any synergies with other ingredients?
8. Any compliance concerns with claims?

### FOR ALL PRODUCTS: Mechanism Research

### Step 4: Research the Core Mechanism
**[TIME: 1-2 hours]**

Whatever your product, research what makes it work:

#### Research Questions
1. What's the science/logic behind the approach?
2. Why does this method work when others don't?
3. What research supports this approach?
4. Are there case studies or documented results?
5. What experts endorse this methodology?
6. What's the counter-intuitive insight?

#### Mechanism Research Template
```markdown
## Mechanism Research

### The Core Approach
[Description of the methodology/mechanism]

### Why It Works (Research/Logic)
[Evidence and reasoning]

### Supporting Research
| Research Type | Finding | Source |
|---------------|---------|--------|
| [Study/Case/Logic] | [What it shows] | [Source] |

### Expert Support
| Expert | Credentials | What They Say |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| [Name] | [Credentials] | "[Quote/Position]" |

### Comparison to Alternatives
| Approach | Why It Fails/Succeeds | Evidence |
|----------|----------------------|----------|
| [Traditional approach] | [Why it fails] | [Evidence] |
| [Our approach] | [Why it works] | [Evidence] |

### The "Aha" Explanation
[How to explain this simply so anyone gets it]
```

## AI Agent Instructions

### Agent: Scientific Research Agent

**Trigger:** Product requires scientific backing

**Instructions:**
```
You are a Scientific Research Agent for copywriting projects. Your job is to
research products, ingredients, and mechanisms to support copy claims.

When given a product to research:

1. IDENTIFY what needs research:
   - Core mechanism/methodology
   - Individual ingredients (supplements)
   - Scientific backing for claims
   - Expert support

2. RESEARCH systematically:
   - Use credible sources (PubMed, peer-reviewed journals)
   - Note clinically effective dosages
   - Find key studies
   - Identify expert opinions

3. SYNTHESIZE findings:
   - Create clear mechanism explanations
   - Document evidence for each claim
   - Note compliance considerations
   - Identify unique angles

4. TRANSLATE to copy applications:
   - Simple explanations for consumers
   - Proof points to emphasize
   - Stories and analogies to use

OUTPUT:
Complete Product Research Document (markdown format)

CRITICAL REQUIREMENTS:
- All claims must have evidence
- Note compliance restrictions
- Include source citations
- Provide consumer-friendly explanations
- Identify unique/interesting findings
```
