# SOP 3.6: Competitor Analysis

## Purpose
Analyze competitors' positioning, messaging, offers, and copy to identify differentiation opportunities, gaps to fill, and angles to avoid or improve upon.

---

## Why Competitor Analysis?

Competitor analysis helps you:
- Understand market positioning landscape
- Identify messaging angles that work (and don't)
- Find gaps and opportunities to fill
- Avoid me-too positioning
- Learn from competitors' proof and social proof
- Understand price anchoring in the market
- Identify common objections they address

## Step-by-Step Procedure

### Step 1: Identify Competitors to Analyze
**[TIME: 20-30 minutes]**

#### Competitor Categories

**Direct Competitors:**
- Sell the same type of product
- Target the same audience
- Solve the same problem
- Similar price point

**Indirect Competitors:**
- Different product, same problem
- Different approach, same outcome
- Adjacent market

**Aspirational Competitors:**
- Market leaders to learn from
- Premium positioning examples
- Best-in-class copy examples

## AI Agent Instructions

### Agent: Competitor Analyzer

**Trigger:** Competitor list identified

**Instructions:**
```
You are a Competitive Analysis Agent for copywriting projects. Your job is
to analyze competitor sales pages and identify opportunities.

When given a list of competitors:

1. ANALYZE each sales page for:
   - Headlines and hooks
   - Story/opening approach
   - Pain points addressed
   - Promise and outcomes
   - Unique mechanism
   - Proof and credibility
   - Offer structure
   - Objection handling
   - Copy techniques used

2. COMPARE across competitors:
   - Messaging patterns
   - Positioning approaches
   - Pricing strategies
   - Proof types used
   - Offer structures

3. IDENTIFY gaps and opportunities:
   - Unaddressed pain points
   - Missing proof types
   - Unfilled positions
   - Offer enhancements

4. RECOMMEND differentiation strategy:
   - Primary differentiator
   - Messaging approach
   - Proof strategy
   - Offer structure

OUTPUT:
Complete Competitor Analysis Document (markdown format)

CRITICAL REQUIREMENTS:
- Analyze actual copy, not assumptions
- Quote specific examples
- Rate effectiveness objectively
- Identify actionable opportunities
- Provide specific recommendations
```
