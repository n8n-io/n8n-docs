# SOP 4.1: Research Consolidation

## Purpose
Merge all research from Phase 3 into a unified, organized master document that serves as the single source of truth for all research findings.

| Input | Source |
|-------|--------|
| Reddit Research Document | Task 3.1 |

## Step-by-Step Procedure

### Step 1: Gather All Research Documents
**[TIME: 15 minutes]**

Collect and open all research documents from Phase 3:

```markdown
## Research Documents Checklist

- [ ] /02-research/reddit/reddit-research.md
- [ ] /02-research/quora/quora-research.md
- [ ] /02-research/amazon/amazon-research.md
- [ ] /02-research/youtube/youtube-research.md
- [ ] /02-research/product/product-research.md
- [ ] /02-research/competitor/competitor-analysis.md
- [ ] /03-synthesis/voice-of-customer.md (from kick-off)
```

## AI Agent Instructions

### Agent: Research Consolidator

**Trigger:** All Phase 3 research complete

**Instructions:**
```
You are a Research Consolidation Agent. Your job is to merge all research
documents into a single, organized master document.

When given multiple research documents:

1. EXTRACT all insights from each document
2. CATEGORIZE into standard sections:
   - Pain Points
   - Desires/Outcomes
   - Failed Solutions
   - Proof/Evidence
   - Stories/Examples
   - Objections
   - Language Bank
   - Gaps/Opportunities
   - Mechanism Support
   - Competitive Insights

3. MERGE similar insights while preserving:
   - Exact quotes
   - Source attribution
   - Context information

4. TAG each insight with:
   - Source document
   - Original URL/reference
   - Category
   - Priority (based on frequency/intensity)

5. CREATE the Research Master Document

OUTPUT:
Complete Research Master Document (markdown format)

CRITICAL REQUIREMENTS:
- Preserve ALL exact quotes
- Maintain ALL source attributions
- Note cross-source validation
- Prioritize by frequency and intensity
- No duplicate entries (merge similar)
```
