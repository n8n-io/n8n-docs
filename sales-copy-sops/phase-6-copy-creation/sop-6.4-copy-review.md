# SOP 6.4: Copy Review & Refinement

## Purpose
Review and refine all copy to ensure quality, coherence, and effectiveness before client delivery.

---

## Review Checklist

### Strategic Review

- [ ] **Target Audience:** Does this speak to the ideal customer?
- [ ] **Pain Points:** Are the right pains addressed?
- [ ] **Desires:** Are the right desires promised?
- [ ] **Mechanism:** Is the unique mechanism clear?
- [ ] **Proof:** Is there sufficient proof?
- [ ] **Offer:** Is the offer irresistible?
- [ ] **CTA:** Are CTAs clear and compelling?

### Structure Review

- [ ] **Flow:** Does the copy flow logically?
- [ ] **Transitions:** Are transitions smooth?
- [ ] **Length:** Is each section appropriately sized?
- [ ] **Pacing:** Does energy build appropriately?
- [ ] **Hero's Journey:** Does reader feel taken on a journey?

### Voice & Tone Review

- [ ] **Conversational:** Does it sound like talking to a friend?
- [ ] **Customer Language:** Is VOC language used?
- [ ] **Consistent Tone:** Is tone consistent throughout?
- [ ] **Brand Voice:** Does it match client's brand?

### Formatting Review

- [ ] **Readability:** Short sentences and paragraphs?
- [ ] **White Space:** Enough breathing room?
- [ ] **Subheads:** Breaking up content effectively?
- [ ] **Bold/Emphasis:** Used appropriately?
- [ ] **Bullets:** Lists are scannable?

### Compliance Review

- [ ] **Claims:** All claims substantiated?
- [ ] **Disclaimers:** Required disclaimers included?
- [ ] **Testimonials:** Properly attributed/disclosed?
- [ ] **Income/Results:** Appropriate caveats?
- [ ] **Platform Policies:** Ad copy compliant?

---

## Line-by-Line Editing

### Tighten Writing
- Remove unnecessary words
- Eliminate passive voice
- Cut repetition
- Strengthen weak verbs

### Enhance Impact
- Replace weak words with power words
- Add sensory language
- Strengthen emotional appeals
- Sharpen specificity

### Check Clarity
- One idea per paragraph
- Clear antecedents
- No jargon without explanation
- Logical flow

---

## Pre-Delivery Checklist

- [ ] All copy sections complete
- [ ] Proofread for typos/grammar
- [ ] Links and placeholders filled
- [ ] Formatting consistent
- [ ] File naming correct
- [ ] Client requirements met
